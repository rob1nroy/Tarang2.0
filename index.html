AboutUs

In 2001, the laudable adventure of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering began. The department has always been on the fast track to advancement because to its first-rate facilities and fully qualified professors, making it one of Amal Jyothi's flagship departments. Without E & C Engineering, everything from a pricey smart phone to satellite communication would be lacking in our life. We educate our students in the research, development, and manufacture of electronic devices in order to satisfy the needs of the equipment and components in daily life. We also provide them a comprehensive and realistic view of the emerging digital world. The department has taken satisfaction in preparing students for success throughout their lives from the start and anticipates seeing this trend continue in the years to come.
