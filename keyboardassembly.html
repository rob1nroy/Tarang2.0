KEYBOARD ASSEMBLY





Rules and Regulation


1. Registration : Any college student can participate.
2. Brief Description : Open for all.
3. Prerequisite :
4. Mode : Max of 2 players per team can participate.
5. Registration Fee : Rs 50/-
6. Challenge format : Place the keys in their respective positions with a max of 15 tries for 15 key placements. Each placing will be reviewed by the team and will undo the placing but the tries count on. Final score will be the number of correct placements after 15 tries. In case if highest score is tied between teams the prize will be shared among them equally.
7. Time limit : Time limit will be max of 4 min for each team.
8. Disqualification: Any misconduct will lead the team to be disqualified.



PRIZES : Prizes worth 1.5k
*College ID is mandatory !
Winners and Runner Ups will be provided with cash prize and certificates.
