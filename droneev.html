Rules and regulations:
1. Any college student having a drone can participate.
2. Each participant should fly three laps.The winner will be the person who completes a lap within the minimum time duration.
3. The line of sight will be flying.
4. The usage of FPV is not permitted.
5. Drones having a propeller size of 5 inch or above must be used.
6. The minimum obstructive size is 1 metre length and 1 metre height.
7. The batteries used for the drones should be of 6 cell or below.
