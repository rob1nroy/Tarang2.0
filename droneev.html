DRONE RACING COMPETITION


Are you ready to set your drone racing skills into action??? Drone Racing is an exciting and rapidly growing sport that combines the thrill of high-speed racing with the agility and manoeuvrability of a drone. It requires a balance of spatial skills, hand-to-eye coordination and creative vision. Three laps per participant are required. The individual who completes the lap in the minimum time will be the winner. Both flyers and spectators will have a unforgettable experience. Compete with your friends for an amazing experience and rewards. Prepare yourself to pitch, yaw and roll a drone. and win exciting prize!!

PRIZES :
Prizes worth 20K.
Rules and regulation

1. Registration: Any college student having a drone can participate.
2. Each participant should fly three laps. The winner will be the person who completes a lap within the minimum time duration.
3. The line of sight will be flying.
4. The usage of FPV is not permitted.
5. Drones having a propeller size of 5 inch or above must be used.
6. The minimum obstructive size is 1 meter length and 1 meter height.
7. The batteries used for the drones should be of 6 cell or below.
8. Mode : Individual.
9. Only for external students.

Registration Fee : Rs.750 Per head.
